Refer a Friend

Did you know you can earn £250 by referring friends or family to us?

Do you enjoy working for Servoca Nursing and Care and wish you could share all of the excellent benefits you get with your friends and family? Who doesn’t want shifts where and when suits them! By referring your friends and family to us can earn you £250, and they will get £75.

Our ‘Refer a Friend’ scheme is our way of thanking you for bringing great candidates to us. You can earn up to £250 for each person you refer and what’s better is you can refer as many people to us as you like and they also get rewarded.

How it Works

  • Tell your friends and family about working with us
  • Fill in their details via the message box in the form below or email referafriend@servocahealth.com
  • They start to work with us and complete a minimum number of shifts
  • We will pay you your reward (see T&Cs below)
  • You can continue to refer as many people as you like
  • Before providing your friends details, you must get their permission to provide their personal information to us. We will inform them that the recommendation came from you when we contact them.
