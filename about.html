Careers With Us

Careers at Servoca Nursing and Care An opportunity to show your true potential!

At Servoca Nursing and Care and Servoca Complex Care, a career with us is much more than just a job, our consultants run their desks as they would their own business – we encourage ownership & accountability and believe passionately in empowering our people to perform.

Unlike many recruitment companies, we invest in your for the long-term and our consultants average four years with us!

Be a part of our team

We are always on the lookout for exceptional individuals, from trainees, through to aspiring (or current) branch managers, who want to work in an environment promoting autonomy, trust and empowerment. In return we offer a supportive place to work with a range of excellent benefits, rewards and opportunities. Please get in touch with us if you would like to discuss an opportunity by emailing jointheteam@servocahealth.com.

Reward and Benefit

  • Pension scheme
  • Competitive salary
  • Apprenticeship and continuous professional development schemes
  • Career progression
  • Annual social events
  • Well-being services, including specialist mental health support
  • Commission structure – that is uncapped!
  • Potential to grow your own team in a simple, structured way. No complex progression structure, put simply – if you deliver, so will we!
