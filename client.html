Leading provider of healthcare staff across the UK. Providing you with high-quality healthcare workers when you need them.

Servoca Nursing and Care is a UK provider of temporary and permanent nursing, care and support workers. We have over 20 years experience of providing nurses, healthcare assistants and support workers throughout the UK, with an excellent reputation and proven track record in swiftly providing experienced, qualified and well trained staff.

We understand clients often require temporary staff at short notice. Our ability to respond quickly to urgent requirements is a cornerstone of our service.

Who Do We Serve?

  • Nursing homes
  • Care homes
  • Residential homes
  • Support settings include learning disabilities, autism and complex needs.
  • Mental health units

    • Meeting your Needs

    We know that healthcare can be fast paced and unpredictable and understand that there will always be last minute emergencies that require the supply of healthcare professionals. Our team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to provide both planned and emergency staffing solutions.

    We pride ourselves on providing continuity of care, on-going professional development for our care givers and an industry leading screening and compliance process. With a strong network of healthcare professionals, we place candidates every week into private care home/nursing homes across the UK. We specialise in the provisions of temporary healthcare staff including:

    • Registered general nurses
    • Senior carers
    • 1-2-1 care
    • Healthcare assistants
    • Support workers
    • Mental health nurses

    Quality and Reliable Staffing Solutions

    Our in-house recruitment team employ a strict, professional and well-structured approach when recruiting for candidates. The central recruitment team will search for, and interview only the most suitable applicants for the role and will conduct face-to-face interviews, therefore ensuring only the highest quality of candidates for our clients.

    Our dedicated compliance team pride themselves in taking candidates through the registration process as quickly and efficiently as possible, ensuring they are fully compliant and vetted before they are able to work.
